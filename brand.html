Key rules

  • Body text → always Teal Ink (#1F3536) on Sand. Ratio 10.1:1 — passes AAA.
  • Teal (#3F8487) and Clay (#C75A2A) fail AA for body text on Sand. Use them only on large headings (≥18px bold or ≥24px), UI elements, and decorative accents.
  • Clay buttons pass AA for large/UI text (3:1) when using bold, uppercase, and sufficient padding.
  • Teal slab backgrounds require light text at large sizes. For body text on teal, use a darker teal variant or increase font weight.
  • Do not place Clay text on Teal backgrounds or vice versa — contrast drops below 2:1.
Proper Usage

Brand guidelines

These rules protect the integrity and recognizability of the Tellus brand. Follow them whenever you use our name, logo, or colors in any medium.

Do

  • Use the official logo files from this page — never recreate or trace the wordmark.
  • Maintain a clear exclusion zone around the logo equal to the height of the letter "T".
  • Use the dark logo on light/neutral backgrounds and the white logo on dark backgrounds.
  • Follow the contrast rules above — body text must be Teal Ink (#1F3536) on Sand (#ECE0CC).
  • Use the brand typefaces: Fraunces for headings, Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for data/code.
  • When referring to us in text, write "Tellus Cooperative" or "Tellus Cooperative Foundation" on first mention.
  • Link back to telluscoop.org when using our brand assets online.

Don't

  • Stretch, compress, rotate, or distort the logo in any way.
  • Change the logo colors — use the approved variants only.
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the logo.
  • Place the logo on busy, low-contrast, or clashing backgrounds.
  • Use Clay (#C75A2A) or Teal (#3F8487) for body text — they fail WCAG AA at normal sizes.
  • Create your own lockups, sub-brands, or derivative logos without written permission.
  • Use the Tellus brand to imply endorsement, partnership, or sponsorship without our explicit consent.

Clear space & minimum size

The logo must be surrounded by a minimum clear space equal to the height of the letter T in the wordmark. No other elements — text, images, borders — may enter this zone.

Minimum print size: 30 mm width. Minimum digital size: 120 px width. Never scale below these thresholds.
